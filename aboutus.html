Ntroducing the Visionary CEO Behind the Sri lankan First-Ever Aquascape Concept:


Meet founder Buddika Kadurugamuwa, CEO of Blackstone Engineering , an icon in landscape innovation, poised to reshape outdoor aesthetics. With a career defined by boundless creativity, Buddika now pioneers a groundbreaking venture – the world's inaugural aquascape concept.


For Mr. Kadurugamuwa, the fusion of aquatic and terrestrial realms isn't merely design; it's a philosophy. The aquascape concept intertwines water and land, crafting immersive environments where aquatic life and lush plant arrangements coexist harmoniously.


This innovation beckons us to rediscover nature's wonders – waterfalls flowing into tranquil pools, underwater ecosystems dancing with carefully sculpted landscapes. Kadurugamuwa's visionary and dedication to redefining landscaping inspire us all.


Join us in exploring the genesis and principles of Mr. Kadurugamuwa's aquascape concept, an odyssey into a future where nature's essence thrives anew.

About Lotuscape

Welcome to Lotuscape, where nature's serenity meets artistic ingenuity. Our passion lies in reimagining outdoor spaces as vibrant aquatic landscapes that captivate the senses and rejuvenate the soul. Founded with a vision to harmonize the beauty of aquatic ecosystems and the art of landscaping, Lotuscape is at the forefront of the aquascape revolution. As pioneers of this innovative concept, we blend the elegance of water with the artistry of design, creating enchanting environments that redefine the boundaries of traditional landscaping. With every project, we strive to craft immersive experiences that celebrate the delicate balance between aquatic life, lush flora, and sculpted hardscapes. At Lotuscape, our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and the boundless wonders of nature drives us to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary aquascapes. Join us on this journey as we create living works of art that inspire, heal, and connect us to the tranquil beauty of the aquatic world.
